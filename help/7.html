Stock Control

"Stock Control": This function is used to control your inventory.
You can locate the stock item that are being viewed or edited, or;
Generate a weekly report for transactions in the current week.

To change stock details:

1. First, locate the item that you wish to edit:

2. Then, double click on the item to open details window:


3. Simple change the fields that need to be adjusted using the spin box ()
Note: Sold stock is not editable.

4. After finish editing, click on "Save" and your changes will be saved. Or;
Click on "Return" to ignore the changes and go back to the Stock Control screen.
