Making a Purchase

The "Item Details" screen is what you see after an item has been clicked.
In this screen, you can choose the quantity to be added or to return to the previous screen.

2. Selecting Item

To select the intended quantity of the item, use the spin box () as labelled in (1).
After the amount has been selected, click "Add to Cart" to add the items into the shopping cart.


After your intended item has been successfully added to the shopping cart as the notification suggests, proceed to Reviewing Cart

If you changed your mind about the item or has clicked the incorrect one, click "Return" to go back to the previous screen
